Program Section

There are three programs in the international hockey federation: Junior program, Teenager program and Professional program. Players are categorized mainly on the basis of age and sometimes skills

Junior Program

The junior program of the Hockey Federation focuses on developing young talent, imparting skills, fostering teamwork, and instilling a passion for the sport among aspiring players.

Teenager Program

The teenage hockey program by the federation focuses on honing skills, fostering teamwork, and instilling discipline. Participants undergo rigorous training, competitions, and mentorship, shaping future hockey stars.

Professional Program

The professional program of the hockey federation emphasizes skill development, strategic training, and team collaboration. Athletes undergo rigorous coaching to enhance performance, ensuring competitiveness and success on the ice.
